My Story

How I Went From ___ to ____

For most of my life, I've struggled with my own anxiety and digestive issues. No matter what I tried, I was left feeling burnt out and not quite right in my own body.

I needed to stop and slow down.

Too many times I found myself on my bedroom floor crying and writing in pain. My chest killing me. Stabbing pains in my stomach. Trouble breathing.

My body was struggling, and she was crying out to me for help. She was begging me to listen and take better care of her.

I wanted to give up. I felt like my anxiety, pains, and digestive issues had taken control over my life.

Enter, my self love journey.

One morning, I woke up, looked in the mirror, and promised myself that things were going to change for the better. I needed a long-term, life-changing solution.

And that's when it clicked. When I realized how much my mind and my stomach were connected, I was able to learn how to truly feel better

I realized that my purpose is to give that to other women finding themselves in the same situation.

I empower, support, embrace, and encourage my clients every step of the way as they cultivate a healthy relationship with food, their bodies, and themselves.
